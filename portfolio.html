About me

I'm a telecommunications engineer (telematics) who graduated with a 10/10 thesis on IPv6 security with Secure Neighbor Discovery. I'm currently pursuing two master's degrees in parallel: Cybersecurity at the University of Seville and Telecommunications Engineering at UNIR.

My MSc thesis analyses how well MITRE ATT&CK Enterprise network techniques can be detected via HIDS. This means I'm actively deploying, configuring, and testing Wazuh, OSSEC, Samhain, and VelociRaptor in virtualised labs — writing custom rules, correlating events, and validating against documented ATT&CK tactics. Not following a tutorial. Actually doing it.

My telecoms foundation isn't a footnote — it's an edge. Understanding BGP, MPLS, IPSec, and IPv6 at a deep level means I can interpret a network alert from the packet upward. I build context, not just dashboards.
